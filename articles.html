My Writings.

My high school, a National School of Character, has taught me so much about the greatnesses of mind, heart, and character. These greatnesses not only resonate with me through my enthusiasm for learning, kindness to all, and my growth mindset, but also with my desire to inspire others to follow the greatness in themselves. Throughout my senior year of high school, I wrote articles about purpose and wellness in my school’s newspaper (The Looking Glass Newspaper) as the Wellness Section Editor and in my school’s LifeCompass blog.
